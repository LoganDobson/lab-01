The Biography

In 2002 I was born in Collingwood Ontario, I currently live in Collingwood as well and i commute to attend my classes. I grew up in Creemore Ontario, (you probably havent heard of it) It is a small town near Stayner and is most known for Creemore Springs.

What schools have I attended?

Since i grew up in Creemore I attended the local Elementary school NCPS which stands for Nottawasaga Creemore Public School. It is a very Small school of about 100-200 students. At the end of grade 5 my family moved to Brampton, The School I attended was called Williams Parkway Sr. Public School. It was a huge difference as it had about 1400 students and went from grade 6-8. For High School my family migrated north again and moved to Collingwood, I attended Collingwood Collegiate Institute for my high school which I graduated in 2020.

I am hoping to achieve much more during my years attending this school and afterwards once I graduate.
